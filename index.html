About Us

Afripriemer Group is a trusted Kenyan provider offering end-to-end solutions in General Supplies, Procurement Consultancy, Business Advisory, and AI & Robotics. We simplify procurement processes and power business growth for public institutions and private organizations through professionalism, reliability, and value.

With a customer-first approach, we ensure every product delivered and every strategy recommended is geared toward real-world impact and lasting results.
